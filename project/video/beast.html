Beast

Beast is a 2022 Indian Tamil-language action dark comedy film[4] written and directed by Nelson Dilipkumar, and produced by Sun Pictures. The film stars Vijay, alongside Pooja Hegde, Selvaraghavan, Shaji Chen, VTV Ganesh, Ankur Vikal, Aparna Das, Sathish Krishnan, Shine Tom Chacko, Yogi Babu and Redin Kingsley.
